Resonate — Support

Resonate is a heart-coherence breathing app for iPhone and Apple Watch.

Getting started

  • Open the app and grant Health access when prompted (needed for the live heart rate and coherence score).
  • For the full experience, pair an Apple Watch and use headphones.
  • Tap to begin a session; follow the breath guidance and watch your coherence rise.

Frequently asked

  • Why does it need Health access? To read your heart rate and HRV in real time and show your coherence. This data stays on your device.
  • Do I need an Apple Watch? The Watch provides the most accurate live heart rate, but the app also works on iPhone alone.
  • Do I need headphones? They're recommended for the audio feature, but optional.
  • Is my data shared? No. Health data never leaves your device and is never sent to a server. See our Privacy Policy.
